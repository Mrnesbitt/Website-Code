Honorable Mention

GNX – Kendrick Lamar

  • Wacced Out Murals
  • The Heart Pt. 6
  • Man at the Garden
  • Reincarnated
  • Tv Off
  • Luther

For me, Kendrick’s album was exactly what I needed. It feels like the baton being passed off to the fourth leg of a relay—everything depends on this, and the energy is right there. It has this I’ve come so far, and I’m about to pop out energy that’s undeniable. The album feels monumental, a reflection of progress and purpose. I liked a lot of songs, and overall, the project left a lasting impression. Kendrick delivered something powerful, meaningful, and deeply inspiring. It absolutely had to make the list.
