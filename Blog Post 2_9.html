4.Vultures 1 – Kanye West

  • Back to Me
  • Talking
  • Paperwork
  • Hood Rat
  • Carnival
  • King
  • Vultures
  • Beg Forgiveness

Kanye was my top artist of the year, but this album didn’t quite outshine the competition. Still, he’s a master at his craft, delivering standout tracks with sharp lyricism and memorable production. With features like Ty Dolla $ign, this album is full of songs that linger with you long after they’re over. Kanye’s delivery remains top-tier, even when the project feels a little uneven.
