My Favorite Albums of 2024

    1. Megan Act I & Act II - Megan Thee Stallion

  • TYG
  • Hot Girl Otaku
  • Bigger in Texas
  • Rattle
  • B.A.S.
  • Worthy
  • Cobra
  • Like a Freak
  • Bourbon
  • Right Now
  • Find Out

I’ve been a big Megan fan for years, following her journey since her YouTube freestyle days. Listening to this album feels like witnessing the pinnacle of her artistry—a perfect fusion of her talent, charisma, and personal growth. The storytelling throughout the album is captivating. Megan lays bare her struggles with fake friends, industry standards, and the betrayal of those who turned against her at her lowest moments. What sets this album apart is its bold experimentation. From K-pop-inspired sections to old-school hip-hop beats reminiscent of legends like Biggie Smalls and Tony Snow, Megan effortlessly blends genres. She even ventures into intense rock-hip-hop hybrids, evoking the energy of 2000s nightcore or Linkin Park. Beneath the flashy production and playful innuendos lies a deeper narrative—a story of resilience and self-empowerment. Megan emerges as a woman who has overcome immense challenges, standing tall against the odds. Her relentless work ethic shines through: not only did she release two albums, but she also delivered a feature film, proving her versatility and dedication. Both albums are distinct yet cohesive, forming a vivid portrait of her journey. Megan’s ability to channel her experiences into art is nothing short of inspiring. This project is unapologetically her, and for that reason, it claims the top spot on my list.
