About Us

Welcome to SimpleButComplex, a publishing house dedicated to empowering authors to create their own stories. We believe that storytelling shapes minds and that every voice deserves to be heard.

Not many people take the leap to write books, and even fewer Black authors get the opportunity to share their narratives. Our mission is to change that by fostering creativity, representation, and generational wealth through literature. We want to inspire and uplift through stories—bringing fun, imagination, and fantasy to life.

Whether you're an established creator or just starting, you are welcome here. We support storytellers of all sizes and are committed to providing a platform where creativity thrives.

This initiative is called the Street Comics Initiative—a movement to help authors bring their work to the world on their terms.
