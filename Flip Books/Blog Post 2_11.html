Honorable Mention

Doechii – Alligator Bites Never Heal (EP)

  • Wait
  • Catfish
  • Denial Is a River
  • Profit

Okay, technically this isn’t an album—but it’s my blog, and I make the rules! This EP is perfection. I’ve been a fan of Doechii since Spooky Coochie, and she’s always had a distinct vibe that’s hard to define. ABNH is a showcase of her range. Every track feels unique, with poignant messages woven into the fun and creativity of her music. She takes complex concepts and breaks them down into something that’s a blast to rap along to. I love this EP and can’t wait to see what she does next. Standout Tracks: revise this its for my honorable mentions of this album you can also use the context from the track list I just provided
